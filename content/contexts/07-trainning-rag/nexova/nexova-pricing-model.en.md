# Pricing Model

Executive headhunting:
- Standard fee: 22% of the hired role's gross annual salary.
- Billed in three installments: 30% when the search begins, 30% when the
  final shortlist is presented, 40% upon hire.
- Free replacement guarantee if the candidate leaves the role within 6
  months for causes attributable to the selection process.

Customer support outsourcing:
- Monthly fee per agent: between 1,800 and 2,400 USD, depending on the
  required specialization level and support language.
- Minimum contract: 6 months. Early cancellation carries a penalty
  equivalent to one month's fee.
- The client does not pay recruitment or agent turnover costs; those are
  included in the monthly fee.

Corporate training:
- 4-week program: starting at 3,500 USD for groups of up to 15 people.
- 8-week program: starting at 6,200 USD for groups of up to 15 people.
- 12-week program: starting at 8,900 USD for groups of up to 15 people.
- Groups larger than 15 people require a custom quote; there is no
  published fixed rate for large groups.
