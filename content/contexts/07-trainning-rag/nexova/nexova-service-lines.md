# Service Lines

Nexova operates three business lines:

1. Executive and mid-management headhunting: search and selection of
   managerial and director-level profiles. Includes direct search,
   competency-based evaluation, and reference verification. Average
   delivery time: 6 to 8 weeks from briefing to final shortlist.

2. Customer support team outsourcing: Nexova hires, trains, and manages
   support agents who work exclusively for the client, under the client's
   brand. Includes quality supervision and monthly SLA reporting. Minimum
   contractable team size: 5 agents.

3. Corporate training in soft skills and leadership: 4, 8, or 12-week
   programs, delivered in-person, virtually, or hybrid. Includes an initial
   diagnostic, live sessions, and a completion certificate.

Each service line is contracted independently; a client can hire one, two,
or all three.
