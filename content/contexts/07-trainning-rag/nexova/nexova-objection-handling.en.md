# Handling Common Prospect Objections

"22% seems expensive compared to other firms."
Suggested response: explain that the fee includes the 6-month replacement
guarantee and the reference verification process, which reduces the risk of
a bad hire. A discount below 22% must never be offered without Marcos
Ibáñez's approval.

"Can I pay everything at the end, once the candidate starts working?"
Response: the three-installment payment schedule (30/30/40) is standard and
non-negotiable for new clients. Only clients with more than 2 years of
commercial relationship can request an alternative schedule, and it must be
approved by Finance.

"What happens if I'm not satisfied with the shortlist presented?"
Response: Nexova offers one additional round of search at no cost if none
of the candidates in the initial shortlist are acceptable, provided the
client has given specific feedback on why they didn't fit.

"Do you work with companies in my same sector that are direct competitors?"
Response: Nexova does not take on searches for two companies in direct
conflict of interest in the same selection process simultaneously, but may
have worked with competitors in the past. This must be communicated with
transparency, never concealed.
