# Hiring Process SLA

Committed delivery times by search type:

- Executive profile (C-level, VP): 8 weeks average, shortlist of 3
  candidates.
- Managerial profile (director, manager): 6 weeks average, shortlist of 3
  to 4 candidates.
- Specialized technical profile: 5 weeks average, shortlist of 3
  candidates.

These times are averages based on previous successful searches, not a
strict contractual guarantee, unless the contract explicitly specifies
otherwise.

If, 4 weeks into an executive search, no qualified candidate has been
presented, the assigned consultant must escalate the case to Javier Almeida
(Operations Manager) and proactively notify the client with an action plan.

Nexova does not guarantee delivery times for searches with highly
restrictive geographic requirements (for example, a single candidate
available in one specific city).
