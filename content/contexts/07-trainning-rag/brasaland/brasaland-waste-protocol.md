# Waste Control Protocol

Every Brasaland location must log product waste in three categories:
expiration, kitchen error, and unexplained shrinkage (possible theft or
counting error).

Daily procedure:
1. At the close of each shift, the kitchen lead weighs and logs waste by
   category in the operations app.
2. Any waste over 2 kg of meat protein in a single shift requires a
   mandatory explanatory note.
3. Unexplained shrinkage above 3% of an ingredient's weekly inventory
   triggers an automatic alert to the operations supervisor.

Common causes of waste accepted without an additional note:
- Expiration of fresh vegetables (short shelf life, normal in operations).
- Preparation error during new staff training (employee's first 2 weeks).

Causes that require direct escalation to Felipe Guerrero:
- Waste of premium meat protein (tenderloin, ribs) exceeding 5 kg in a week
  at the same location.
- Three consecutive weeks of unexplained shrinkage at the same location.

Operational target: keep total waste below 4% of monthly ingredient cost
per location. Locations exceeding 6% for two consecutive months enter an
improvement plan with Operations.
