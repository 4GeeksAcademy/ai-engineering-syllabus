# Supplier Ordering Procedure

Each location manager is responsible for generating the weekly supplier
order every Monday before 10:00 a.m. (local time).

Supplier categories and order frequency:
- Proteins (beef, chicken): weekly order, 48-hour delivery.
- Vegetables and fruit: twice-weekly order (Monday and Thursday), 24-hour
  delivery.
- Beverages and packaging: biweekly order, 5 business-day delivery.
- Imported sauces and condiments: monthly order, 10 to 15 business-day
  delivery (applies only to items imported into Colombia).

Minimum stock rule: no location should operate with less than 3 days of
main protein inventory. If the system projects that stock will fall below
that threshold before the next scheduled delivery, an emergency order must
be generated, which carries an 8% surcharge over list price.

An emergency order requires approval from Lucía Fernández (Procurement
Manager) if it exceeds 500 USD (or the COP equivalent).
