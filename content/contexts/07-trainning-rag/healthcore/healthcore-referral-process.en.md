# Internal Referral Process

When a primary care physician refers a patient to a specialist within the
HealthCore network, the standard process is:

1. The physician creates the referral in the system with the clinical
   reason and required specialty.
2. The system routes the referral to the clinic with an available
   specialist closest to the patient's preferred location.
3. The patient receives a notification with available appointment options.
4. Upon confirmation, the receiving clinic automatically gets a summary of
   the patient's relevant history before the appointment.

Target completed-referral time: 11 days from creation to confirmed
appointment (this is the company's current average, which it is trying to
reduce — not a published service commitment to patients).

If a patient hasn't received appointment options within 5 business days of
the referral being created, the coordinator must escalate the case directly
to Marcus Reid (Director of Clinical Operations).

Referrals to specialists outside the HealthCore network do not follow this
automated process; they require manual handling and can take significantly
longer.
