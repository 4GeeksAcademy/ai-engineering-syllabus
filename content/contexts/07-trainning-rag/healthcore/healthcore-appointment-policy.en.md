# Appointment and Cancellation Policy

Booking appointments:
- Routine appointment: average availability of 3 to 5 days.
- Same-day appointment: available at most clinics for non-critical urgent
  cases, subject to the on-duty physician's availability.

Cancellation policy:
- Cancelling more than 24 hours in advance: no charge.
- Cancelling less than 24 hours in advance, or no-show: 50 USD (or 40 GBP
  in the UK) charge for private-pay patients.
- Medicare or Medicaid patients: not charged a no-show fee, per
  regulation, but the incident is logged in their record.

Automated reminders: the system sends reminders at 48h, 24h, and 2h before
the appointment. A patient can reschedule directly from the reminder link
up to 4 hours before the appointment.

A patient with 3 no-shows in the last 6 months must be flagged for
coordinator review before booking a new routine appointment (does not apply
to urgent appointments).
