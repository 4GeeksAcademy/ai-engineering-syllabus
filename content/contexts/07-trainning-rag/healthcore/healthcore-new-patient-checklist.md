# New Patient Checklist

Every new patient must complete the following before their first
appointment:

1. Medical history form (available on the portal or on paper at the front
   desk).
2. Identity verification with an official document.
3. Insurance coverage verification (see Document A) or self-pay
   confirmation.
4. Signed informed consent for data processing — this consent differs
   between the United States (under HIPAA) and the United Kingdom (under UK
   GDPR); the coordinator must use the form corresponding to the clinic's
   country.

Documents the patient should bring on the day of the appointment:
- A valid ID document.
- Insurance card (if applicable).
- List of current medications, if warranted by the reason for the visit.

A new patient who hasn't completed the medical history form before the
appointment can still be seen, but the consultation time will be reduced
because the physician will need to complete it during the visit.
