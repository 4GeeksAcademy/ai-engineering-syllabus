# Accepted Insurance Coverage

HealthCore accepts different coverage types depending on the country:

United States (Texas, Florida, Georgia):
- Accepted commercial insurance: Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna,
  UnitedHealthcare.
- Medicare: accepted at all US clinics.
- Medicaid: accepted only at Texas and Florida clinics, not currently at
  Georgia locations.
- Uninsured patients: can pay the "self-pay" rate with a 20% discount if
  paid the same day as the visit.

United Kingdom (London and Manchester):
- HealthCore has a limited NHS contract for a reduced number of monthly
  appointments per clinic (a quota, not unlimited).
- Most UK patients pay privately or through private insurance (Bupa, AXA
  Health).
- An NHS patient must confirm their quota slot in advance; if the monthly
  quota is already full, they must be offered the option of private payment
  or referred back to the standard NHS system.

No coordinator should confirm coverage for an insurer not listed here
without first verifying with the billing team (Tom Callahan).
