# Carrier Coverage

TrackFlow works with different carriers depending on the country:

United States (Los Angeles): UPS, FedEx, and DHL.
- UPS: full national coverage, best rate for heavy packages (over 5 kg).
- FedEx: best delivery time for express shipments within California.
- DHL: the only reliable-coverage option for international shipments from
  Los Angeles.

Spain (Zaragoza): MRW, SEUR, DHL, and two local last-mile carriers in the
Zaragoza metropolitan area.
- SEUR: best coverage in rural areas of Aragón.
- MRW: most competitive rates for small packages (under 2 kg).
- Local carriers: only available within the Zaragoza metropolitan area, do
  not cover the rest of Spain.

Carrier selection for each shipment is determined by the system based on
destination, weight, product type, and urgency — the account manager does
not manually choose the carrier except for an exception approved by Carlos
Vega (Head of Carrier Operations).
