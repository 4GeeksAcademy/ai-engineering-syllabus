# Storage Pricing

TrackFlow charges client brands for storage based on volume occupied, not
number of units:

- Standard rate: 18 USD per cubic meter per month (Los Angeles) or 16 EUR
  per cubic meter per month (Zaragoza).
- The first 30 days of any new product in inventory incur no storage
  charge (grace period).
- Inventory with more than 180 days of no movement (no sales or outbound)
  pays a "long-term inventory" fee of 150% over the standard rate, starting
  on day 181.

The client can request an inventory aging report at any time through their
account manager; this report is not currently automated for the end
client, only internally.

Clients with more than 50 cubic meters of average monthly volume can
negotiate a preferential rate, but that negotiation must always go through
Miguel Torres — an account manager cannot offer storage discounts on their
own.
