# Delivery SLA

TrackFlow manages last-mile delivery in the United States (Los Angeles) and
Spain (Zaragoza). Delivery commitments vary depending on the service type
contracted by the client brand:

- Standard shipping: 3 to 5 business days within the same country.
- Express shipping: 24 to 48 hours within the same country (available only
  in the Los Angeles metropolitan area and the Zaragoza-Madrid corridor).
- International shipping (between the United States and Spain): 7 to 12
  business days, subject to customs; TrackFlow does not control customs
  hold times.

The contractually committed SLA is 90% on-time deliveries per month,
measured by country. If a country falls below 90% for two consecutive
months, the client is entitled to a 5% compensation on that country's
monthly fee, per the standard contract.

TrackFlow does not guarantee delivery SLA during declared high-demand dates
(Black Friday, Christmas, January Sales in Spain) — during those dates,
delivery times may extend by up to an additional 40%, and this must be
communicated proactively to clients in advance.
